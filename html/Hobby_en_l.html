Childhood Hobbies

Stamps

Many in childhood collected stamps. I also did not get away from this. My theme was technology - airplanes, cars, steam locomotives, ships. All that moved, puffed, rattled, smoked. But it was not a fanatical hobby. Just went shopping with friends "Philately" and kiosks "Soyuzpechat" and bought stamps with pocket money. It has not grown into something more serious.

Badges

Collecting badges was also in my childhood what attracted probably all children. We exchanged them, searched for shops and kiosks. I remember my sister had a huge collection of badges with images of all medals and orders of the USSR, and in full size. What distinguished them only was that they were badges. Although outwardly almost indistinguishable. How did we envy her.

Sciences

The favorite sciences have always been the exact sciences (mathematics, physics), except chemistry. I never loved chemistry. Still very fond of history. I read a lot of books, both on Russian and foreign history. The love of history has survived to this day. However, like the love of exact sciences. Hence the desire to find time and fully learn programming.

Sport

Sport is in last place. So it was. I tried various sports - boxing, swimming. Football and hockey - played in the yard like everyone else, but there wasn’t much love for these kinds of sports. I always wanted to play tennis, but when I was a teenager, it was a sport for the elite, and then it was just not up to it. As a result, all sports ended - weights, barbell and other henchmen materials that you can do at home in your free time. Although in childhood he still ran quite well, including skiing.

А что сейчас?

All hobbies of childhood have passed. Stamps in stamps albums stacked on mezzanines. There are also boxes with badges. Of the children's hobbies, only sport in the form of homework on simulators andlove of science and history. There was still a hobby for photography - even at one time (when it was difficult to buy a good magnifier) ​​I assembled it with my own hands. This mysterious process of developing film and photographs — it was a miracle — when photographs suddenly appeared on empty photographic paper. Now the digitized pictures - a digital camera, a printer for printing photos and everything is ready - that's not it. Therefore, now all hobbies are knowledge and study of the new, especially in those areas in which I work or in related fields. I’m studying all the new trends in project management (which is exactly what I’m doing). Now I’m interested in Agile methodology, I study both the methods themselves (Scrum, Kanban, Lean and others) and the principles, as well as the techniques of situational leadership and facilitation that accompany them . I evaluate my previous experience in project management, I evaluate how I could solve certain problems using these methods and techniques. It also attracted me because I had previously tried to use certain methods and principles in projects (dividing projects into short stages with the result, building relationships with people according to the situation, focusing the team on the result as the main indicator of work and others) just did not know what it's called. Therefore, it has become a new interest and hobby. The second is English , I think also Spanish. I studied English a lot before, but in everyday life I didn’t really need it, and conversational practice was gone. I regret that I did not pay so much attention to the language before. Now this is a new hobby, which I devote my free time to. And programming. I always wanted to learn programming . Ever since school, when they studied basic and pascal. But then the routine tightened. And in the last few years I have to manage software development projects and I understand that I lack this knowledge. Therefore, I decided to learn Python (as one of the main languages ​​used in working with data), JavaScript (and libraries for it), as well as HTML and CSS . This is also a hobby. Who knows, suddenly this hobby will become the main work, because I like it, and a good basic mathematical preparation allows me to cope with solving various problems. As well as experience in project management. So I share my time - I study new technologies (in terms of artificial intelligence) and project management techniques, programming and foreign languages.
